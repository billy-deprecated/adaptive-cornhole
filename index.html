Adaptive Cornhole

It can be challenging to learn how to play cornhole.

Our adaptive cornhole game gradually adapts its difficulty over time. The idea is to detect how accurate the player is at throwing the sack into the hole and adjust the hole size and the board's distance from the player accordingly.

This allows players to improve their skill level at their own pace, and reduces the manual work they have to do (e.g moving closer or farther). Adding the new parameter of hole size also makes the game play more exciting and adaptive.
